📱 How Pochi la Biashara Works

Student Payment Steps

1
Open M-PESA → Send Money → Send to Mobile Number
2
Enter number: 0701 710 662
3
Confirm name shows SULMAN before paying
4
Enter amount and M-PESA PIN → confirm
5
Send M-PESA confirmation SMS to WhatsApp +254 768 515 494

Your Action After Payment

1
Receive M-PESA notification on 0701710662
2
Verify the amount matches the resource price
3
Go to Orders → click Send Link to WhatsApp the PDF
4
For classes → go to Bookings → click Send Link
📋 WhatsApp Message Template for Students
To purchase a resource on KenyaVault:
1. Send M-PESA to: 0701 710 662 (SULMAN)
2. Amount: KSh [price of resource]
3. WhatsApp your M-PESA confirmation to: +254 768 515 494
4. Include the name of the resource you want
5. Your PDF download link will be sent within 5 minutes ⚡

⏳ KCB Paybill — Future Integration

⚠️ Automated M-PESA Paybill (522522 / Account 1197966080) requires Safaricom Daraja API setup. This takes 2–4 weeks to activate. We recommend switching to this after your first 50 sales via Pochi la Biashara.
Steps when ready to upgrade:
1. Register at developer.safaricom.co.ke
2. Create an app and get your Consumer Key & Secret
3. Apply for a Business Shortcode linked to your KCB account
4. Paste the credentials in Settings → M-PESA API
5. Payments will then be automated — no manual confirmation needed
