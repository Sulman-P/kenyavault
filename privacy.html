1. Introduction & Scope

KenyaVault ("we," "our," "us") is a comprehensive digital platform that designs, hosts, and distributes curriculum-aligned educational materials for Kenyan schools, teachers, and parents. Our core products are strictly digital, downloadable softcopy resources including Examinations, Lesson Plans, Schemes of Work, Holiday Homework, and Revision Papers.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website kenyavault.co.ke (the "Site") and use our educational resource marketplace. It applies to all users of our platform, including:

  • Individual Educators — Teachers and tutors accessing resources for classroom use
  • Parents & Guardians — Supporting their children's learning at home
  • Institutional Buyers — Schools and educational institutions making bulk purchases
  • Content Creators — Educators who contribute resources to the platform

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with transparency and care. This policy complies with the Kenya Data Protection Act (2019), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) where applicable.

📌 Key Principle: We collect only the information necessary to provide you with our educational resources and services. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

📜 References: Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019 (Sections 25-31); GDPR Articles 4-11; CCPA Sections 1798.100-1798.125

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Provided by Users

When you use KenyaVault, we may collect the following information that you voluntarily provide:

  • Account Registration Details: Full name, email address, phone number, and role (Teacher, Parent, School Administrator, etc.) when you create an account or make a purchase
  • School or Institutional Information: School name, address, and official contact details for institutional buyers and bulk purchases
  • Payment Information: M-PESA transaction codes and payment references (we do not store your M-PESA PIN or full payment credentials)
  • Resource Preferences: Information about the subjects, grade levels, and resources you view, purchase, or download
  • Communications: Messages, inquiries, and feedback you send to us via WhatsApp, email, or our contact forms
  • Creator Information: For educators uploading resources, additional details about your qualifications and teaching experience

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our Site, we automatically collect certain information about your device and usage to enhance security and improve our services:

  • IP Address & Device Type: To prevent fraudulent bulk downloading, detect suspicious activity, and ensure fair usage of our resources
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, resources viewed, search queries, and click patterns to improve user experience
  • Download History: Records of resources you have downloaded to enable automated anti-piracy digital watermarking and maintain your user dashboard history
  • Browser Information: Browser type, operating system, and device type to ensure compatibility and optimize performance
  • Cookies: Small text files stored on your device to enhance your experience and remember your preferences
🔒 We do not collect:
  • Sensitive personal information (e.g., race, religion, health data, political opinions)
  • Financial account numbers, passwords, or full payment credentials
  • Biometric data or precise location tracking
  • Student academic performance data (unless explicitly provided for school subscriptions)

📜 References: Kenya DPA Section 2 (Definitions); GDPR Article 4(1) (Personal Data); CCPA Section 1798.140(o) (Personal Information)

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect for the following specific purposes related to our digital marketplace for educational resources:

  • Delivery of Digital Assets: To process your purchases and deliver downloadable softcopy resources (Examinations, Schemes of Work, Lesson Plans, Holiday Homework, Revision Papers) to your WhatsApp or email
  • Automated Anti-Piracy Digital Watermarks: To protect the intellectual property of our creators, we generate and embed automated digital watermarks on downloaded resources. This process stamps the buying school's name or the individual teacher's email/phone on the document pages to deter unauthorized distribution
  • Download Tracking & User Dashboard: To maintain active user dashboard histories and track unique download permissions, ensuring that teachers and schools can easily retrieve their purchased resources at any time
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries, resolve issues related to resource access or downloads, and provide technical assistance
  • Platform Improvement: To analyze usage patterns, identify popular resources, and enhance the overall user experience on KenyaVault
  • Communication: To send order confirmations, payment receipts, download links, and important updates about our services or resource availability
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes, including anti-piracy and copyright protection
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, unauthorized access, illegal distribution of resources, and other security issues
✅ Your Information, Your Control: We only use your information for the purposes explicitly stated in this policy. We do not use your data for marketing without your explicit consent, and we do not share your personal information with other users or third parties for promotional purposes.

📜 References: Kenya DPA Section 26 (Purpose of Processing); GDPR Article 5(1)(b) (Purpose Limitation); CCPA Section 1798.100(b) (Right to Know)

4. Secure Payment Processing

KenyaVault prioritizes the security of your financial transactions. We utilize industry-standard, third-party payment gateways to process all payments securely:

  • Local Mobile Money: M-PESA STK push transactions are processed directly through trusted payment gateways such as Megapay or Safaricom's official APIs. We do not store your M-PESA PIN or any sensitive financial credentials
  • Credit/Debit Cards: Card payments are processed through encrypted, PCI-DSS compliant gateways like Stripe or Pesapal. All card details are handled by the payment processor and are never stored on our servers
  • Data Encryption: All payment data is transmitted using SSL/TLS encryption, ensuring that your financial information is protected during the transaction process
  • No Raw Financial Data: KenyaVault does not store raw billing or financial data. We retain only transaction references, order histories, and payment confirmations necessary for record-keeping and customer support
🔒 PCI DSS Compliant: Our payment processors adhere to the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS), ensuring the highest level of security for your payment information.

📜 References: Kenya DPA Section 26(2) (Data Security); GDPR Article 32 (Security of Processing); PCI DSS Requirements 3.1-3.4 (Cardholder Data Protection)

5. Institutional & School Data

For schools and educational institutions making purchases or subscribing to our services, we collect and manage data with enhanced security and privacy measures:

  • School Information: We collect institutional details including school name, official contact information, and physical address for billing and resource delivery purposes
  • Teacher Rosters: For school-wide subscriptions, we may manage teacher accounts and access permissions within the institution to ensure proper resource distribution
  • Purchase Histories: We maintain secure records of school-wide purchase histories and subscription details to facilitate renewal and provide ongoing access to resources
  • Access Controls: Institutional accounts have designated administrators who can manage user access and permissions within their school's account
  • Data Protection: All institutional data is protected with the same security measures as individual user data, with additional access controls to prevent unauthorized use
🏫 Institutional Responsibility: Schools are responsible for managing their internal access controls and ensuring that only authorized personnel have access to purchased resources. KenyaVault provides the tools and security infrastructure, but individual schools are responsible for their internal usage policies.

📜 References: Kenya DPA Section 30 (Data Controller Responsibilities); GDPR Article 28 (Processor Contracts); CCPA Section 1798.115 (Business Purposes)

6. Data Retention & User Rights

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, including:

  • Transaction Records: We retain purchase records for tax and accounting purposes as required by Kenyan law (typically 7 years) and to ensure that you can access your purchased resources at any time
  • Download History: Softcopy download history is stored for user retrieval to ensure that teachers and schools don't lose access to their bought Schemes of Work, Lesson Plans, and other resources
  • Customer Support: Communication records are retained for up to 3 years to provide ongoing support and resolve any issues
  • Account Information: If you create an account, we retain your information until you request deletion or the account becomes inactive
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated usage data may be retained indefinitely for analytics and improvement purposes (this data does not identify you personally)

Your Rights Under Kenya DPA, GDPR & CCPA

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: You can correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): You can request that we delete your personal information (subject to legal obligations and legitimate business interests)
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can limit how we use your information
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: You can object to the processing of your data for certain purposes
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw your consent at any time
  • Right to Opt-Out (CCPA): California residents have the right to opt-out of the sale of their personal information (we do not sell data)
📧 To exercise any of these rights: Contact our Data Protection Officer at adminnexalearn@gmail.com with your request. We will respond within 30 days as required by law.

📜 References: Kenya DPA Sections 26-30; GDPR Articles 15-21; CCPA Sections 1798.105-1798.125

7. Data Security Measures

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect it:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our Site is encrypted using SSL/TLS technology. Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256 encryption
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel with a legitimate need have access to personal information. All access is logged and monitored
  • Secure Storage: Your information is stored on secure servers with restricted access, firewalls, and intrusion detection systems
  • Regular Monitoring: We regularly monitor our systems for potential vulnerabilities, security threats, and unauthorized access attempts
  • Data Minimization: We collect only the minimum amount of information necessary to provide our services
  • Incident Response: We have established incident response procedures to quickly address any security breaches
  • Staff Training: All employees and contractors undergo regular privacy and security awareness training
⚠️ Important: While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to continuously improving our security measures.

📜 References: Kenya DPA Section 26(2) (Security Safeguards); GDPR Article 32 (Security of Processing); CCPA Section 1798.100(b) (Reasonable Security)

8. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on KenyaVault:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic Site functionality, such as navigation, shopping cart, and secure transactions
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings to improve your experience
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our Site (e.g., Google Analytics) to improve our services
  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
  • Authentication Cookies: Allow you to stay logged in and access your account and purchased resources

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect Site functionality, including access to purchased resources.

10. Children's Privacy

KenyaVault is intended for use by educators, parents, and educational institutions. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 18 without parental consent.

  • Parental Consent: If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information without your consent, please contact us immediately
  • Parental Monitoring: We encourage parents to monitor their children's online activities and discuss the importance of privacy and online safety
  • Educational Content: Resources on our platform are designed for educational purposes and are appropriate for school-age children under adult supervision
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Parental Guidance: We recommend that parents and educators review resources before children access them to ensure age-appropriateness and alignment with educational goals.

11. International Data Transfers

KenyaVault is based in Kenya, and our services are primarily provided to users in Kenya. However, we may use service providers located in other countries to deliver our services:

  • Supabase: Database and hosting services (servers in the United States and Europe)
  • Stripe/Pesapal: Payment processing (global operations)
  • Google Analytics: Website analytics (global operations)

We ensure that any international data transfers comply with applicable data protection laws by implementing appropriate safeguards, including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs): EU-approved clauses for data transfers
  • Adequacy Decisions: Where applicable, relying on countries with adequate data protection
  • Data Processing Agreements: Contracts with service providers ensuring compliance with data protection laws

By using our Site, you consent to the transfer of your data to these third-party providers as necessary to provide our services.
