About KenyaVault

KenyaVault is Kenya's most comprehensive educational resource platform, serving learners from Preprimary to University. We provide high-quality, curriculum-aligned resources that empower students and educators across the nation.

📚 Our Mission

At KenyaVault, we believe that every learner deserves access to high-quality educational resources. Our mission is to make learning materials accessible, affordable, and aligned with the Kenyan curriculum for every level of education.

We work with experienced educators to create, review, and curate resources that follow the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) and the 8-4-4 system, ensuring that students and teachers have the tools they need to succeed.
