My Story

Why I Built Kenya Vault

I've been a secondary school teacher for over seven years. Every year, I watch students who work hard, attend every class, and genuinely want to succeed — but still struggle in the exam room because they don't have the right revision materials.

After years of sharing handwritten notes with my own students, I decided to package and share them with Kenya — and that's how Kenya Vault was born.

Every resource on this site has been written, refined and tested in a real classroom. These aren't copied from textbooks — they're built from years of teaching experience and understanding exactly where students go wrong.
